Neurological Surgery Milestone

   Apollomedics Super Specialty Hospital is the first hospital in Lucknow to perform brain surgery on a 35-year-old patient suffering from Brain Aneurysm, by stopping heartbeat for 45 seconds, 4 times.
   As a first for Mysore, Endovascular treatment for stroke patients was provided by Apollo BGS Hospitals, Mysore
   South India's first fully functional, Advanced Centre for Movement Disorders was launched by Apollo Hospitals, Chennai.
   A redo of Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS) – A patient with advanced Parkinson’s disease from Mauritius who had DBS done 7 years back had non functional leads. He successfully underwent a redo DBS surgery with good results – a first for Apollo Hospitals.
   Apollo Hospitals has achieved a remarkable milestone of successfully performing over 180,000 neurosurgeries.
